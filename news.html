India's E-Waste Generation Expected to Triple by 2025

According to a recent report, India's e-waste generation is expected to triple by 2025, making it the fifth-largest e-waste producer in the world. The report also highlights the need for proper e-waste management practices to reduce the environmental impact of e-waste.

Apple Commits to Using Only Recycled Materials in Products

Apple has committed to using only recycled materials in its products, including iPhones and MacBooks, by 2030. This is part of the company's efforts to reduce its environmental impact and become carbon neutral by 2030.
